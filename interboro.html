Interboro Partners

Mindich Engaged Fellowship

In Summer 2021, I was an Harvard Mindich Engaged Fellow for Interboro Partners, an architecture firm based in New York City. There, I worked closely with the founder of the firm to develop heat mitigation solutions for the South Bronx. This work consisted of interviews with residents, and with this information creating proposals for public health interventions as well as visuals that can be used to help residents in the future.
